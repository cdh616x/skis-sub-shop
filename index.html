Learn about Ski's

Ski's Sub Shop is a hole-in-the-wall treasure of downtown Grand Rapids. First established in OLD BUILDING, Ski's moved to 96 Monroe Center in YEAR. The quaint restaurant boasts comfortable seating, cable television, and friendly, expert sandwich-makers that enjoy chit-chatting with their patrons.
Owner Pete Cool purchased the shop in YEAR, though he has been making sandwiches here since the shop's inception in 1977. Kelly, Pete's daughter, can be seen working as well, assisting her father in making sandwiches and managing the shop when he is not around.
